563 Carroll Street #1

$2,349,500

This is the unique home oasis that you have been waiting for! A duplex condo with enormous private garden and a private oversized garage.

Enjoy boutique condo living in the heart of Park Slope at 563 Carroll Street, a recently completed project by the Brooklyn Home Company. Enter into the airy great room of residence 1, which boasts a wall of southern facing windows. The sun-drenched space offers room for living and dining areas and is open to the kitchen. Marble waterfall countertops, a huge farmhouse sink, and high end appliances from Viking and Subzero make the kitchen perfect for cooking pros and enthusiasts alike.

The bathrooms feature Peitra Cardosa tile floors, Duravit soaking tubs and Toto toilets, while lighting packages include fixtures from Forecast, Circa and Tradition.

There is no shortage of outdoor space as the home includes a private backyard outdoor space complete with a BBQ area & jacuzzi accessed from the living room area. With open sky to the south and the west, this is your perfect private refuge for outdoor relaxation or entertaining.

Set in prime Park Slope, this outstanding building is surrounded by the best of Brooklyn living. Prospect Park and the Gowanus Whole Foods are within blocks, and access to transportation is unbeatable with N/R and D trains a mere 500 feet away.
