    Rivka Magherefteh

    SELLER’S AGENT

    Driven by results, Rivka’s “won’t settle for less” demeanor is at the forefront in creating alternative solutions, not stubborn standstills. With a passion for tactical approaches in real estate, Rivka delivers reputable results to clients, achieving record breaking deals.

    Dominating New York City Real Estate with Mahgerefteh Advisory, Rivka’s role is to beat with the pulse of the market, negotiating fiercely for her sellers and marketing their properties with the highest quality exposure for local and global recognition. Dynamic communication, organic emotional intelligence, and dedication to win-win solutions come naturally to Rivka. She has absorbed every aspect of the transaction and how to enhance it, growing from intern to salesperson and then luxury broker.

    With a decade of experience in real estate, successfully adding hundreds of millions of dollars in listings to her company portfolio. Following her path of mastery, Rivka has trained dozens of real estate professionals, showing them the whats, the whys, and the hows of real estate.

    Rivka’s ideal client is one who values trust, dedication, and consistency. She’s the respected and highly regarded executor that meticulously uncovers creative solutions for her clients. Rivka is celebrated for holding multiple records, routinely selling above the average price per square foot in multiple buildings throughout New York City, leaving her clients- and colleagues- in awe.
